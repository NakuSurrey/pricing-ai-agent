# Claims Process and Customer Duty

**Policy reference:** HS-CLM-2026
**Applies to:** All Home Shield policies

---

## 1. How to make a claim

Call the claims line on 0800 000 0000 as soon as reasonably possible after
the event. Lines are open 24 hours a day, every day. For non-emergency
claims, use the online claims portal at homeshield.example.com/claims.

When you call, have ready:

- Your policy number
- A short description of what happened
- The date and approximate time of the event
- Photos of the damage, if safe to take them
- A crime reference number if the claim involves theft or malicious damage

## 2. What happens next

- Within 2 working days, a claims handler contacts you to confirm the claim
- For damage claims, a loss adjuster may visit the property within 5
  working days
- Emergency repairs to prevent further damage are authorised by the claims
  handler at the first call
- The full claim decision is issued in writing within 15 working days of
  all evidence being received

## 3. Settlement

Claims are settled on a new-for-old basis for contents less than 5 years
old, unless otherwise stated on the schedule. Buildings claims are settled
on a reinstatement basis, subject to the sum insured being adequate.

If the sum insured is inadequate (underinsurance), the settlement may be
reduced proportionally. This is known as the average clause.

## 4. Disputes

If you disagree with a claim decision, you have the right to:

- Ask for a review by a senior claims manager
- Raise a formal complaint through our complaints process
- Refer the complaint to the Financial Ombudsman Service after 8 weeks if
  unresolved

The Financial Ombudsman Service is a free, independent service for
resolving disputes between consumers and financial firms.

## 5. Our Consumer Duty commitment

Under the FCA Consumer Duty, we commit to:

- Communicate in plain English, free of jargon
- Present product information clearly, fairly, and not misleadingly
- Ensure the product provides fair value for the price paid
- Support customers throughout the life of the policy, especially at
  renewal, claims, and cancellation
- Pay particular attention to customers with characteristics of vulnerability

## 6. Cancellation

You may cancel the policy at any time. If you cancel within 14 days of the
start date or of renewal, you receive a pro-rata refund of the premium,
less a £25 administration fee. After the 14-day cooling-off period,
cancellation is subject to the standard short-period rate table.

## 7. Fraud

Any claim found to be fraudulent, exaggerated, or supported by false
evidence will be declined in full. The policy may be cancelled from the
date of the fraud, previous claim payments may be recovered, and the case
may be reported to the Insurance Fraud Enforcement Department.
