# Standard Homeowner Policy — Home Shield Essential

**Policy reference:** HS-ESS-2026
**Cover type:** Buildings and contents
**Applies to:** Owner-occupied residential property, up to 5 bedrooms

---

## 1. What is covered

The policy covers loss or damage to the insured home caused by:

- Fire, smoke, lightning, and explosion
- Storm and flood (subject to section 4 exclusions)
- Theft and attempted theft, with signs of forced entry
- Escape of water from fixed plumbing
- Impact from vehicles, aircraft, or falling trees
- Subsidence, heave, and landslip, with a £1,000 excess
- Accidental damage to fixed items such as glass and sanitary ware

Contents are covered up to £60,000 as standard. Single-item limit is
£2,000 unless the item is listed as a specified valuable.

## 2. What is not covered

- Wear and tear, gradual deterioration, or lack of maintenance
- Damage caused by pets, rodents, or insects
- Damage while the home is unoccupied for more than 60 consecutive days
- Losses from unforced entry (door left unlocked, window left open)
- Business stock, tools of trade, or income-generating equipment

## 3. Excess

A standard excess of £250 applies per claim. Subsidence claims carry a
£1,000 excess. Escape of water claims carry a £350 excess.

## 4. Flood and storm

Cover applies where the property is in a standard flood-risk zone. For
properties in designated high-risk flood zones, see the Flood Zone Extension
policy. Storm cover excludes damage to fences, gates, and hedges.

## 5. Premium factors

The base premium is adjusted for:

- Property type (detached, semi-detached, terraced, flat)
- Number of bedrooms
- Year of construction — pre-1900 properties attract a loading
- Claims in the last 5 years
- Security features — approved alarm and deadlocks reduce premium
- Postcode risk score from our internal actuarial model

## 6. Renewal

Policies renew annually. Renewal quotes are issued 21 days before expiry.
Customers have the right to cancel within 14 days of renewal at no cost.
