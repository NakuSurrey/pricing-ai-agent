# High-Value Home Policy — Home Shield Prestige

**Policy reference:** HS-PRE-2026
**Cover type:** All-risks buildings and contents
**Applies to:** Properties with rebuild cost above £1,000,000 or contents
value above £150,000

---

## 1. What is covered

The policy is an all-risks policy. That means it covers any sudden and
unexpected loss or damage, unless specifically excluded. Standard cover
includes:

- Fire, flood, storm, escape of water, theft, and subsidence
- Accidental damage throughout the home — no separate extension needed
- Worldwide cover for personal possessions, including jewellery and art
- Cover for fine art, antiques, watches, and collections up to any single
  item limit agreed at inception
- Alternative accommodation up to 3 years if the home becomes uninhabitable
- Guest property cover while guests are staying at the home
- Cover for domestic staff employed at the home

## 2. What is not covered

- Wear, tear, and gradual deterioration
- Damage caused by insects, vermin, or domestic pets
- Mechanical or electrical breakdown of appliances (unless appliance
  breakdown extension is added)
- War, civil commotion, or nuclear risk
- Cyber events — covered only if a cyber extension is added

## 3. Valuables and specified items

Items worth more than £15,000 each must be specified on the schedule and
supported by a professional valuation dated within the last 3 years. Items
not specified are subject to a £15,000 single-item limit.

## 4. Excess

- Standard excess: £1,000
- Subsidence: £2,500
- Jewellery and watches: £500
- No excess applies to fire claims above £50,000

## 5. Risk management requirements

- Approved intruder alarm system, connected to a monitoring centre
- Approved safe for specified valuables above £50,000 total value
- Fire detection on every floor
- Annual review of sums insured

Failure to maintain these may reduce cover or invalidate claims.

## 6. Premium factors

Base premium reflects:

- Rebuild cost, not market value
- Contents sum insured and specified item schedule
- Listed building status
- Location and postcode risk
- Security and fire-protection rating
- Owner's claims history across all insured assets
